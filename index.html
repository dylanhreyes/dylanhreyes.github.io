Hi! My name is Dylan! I'm an audio and broadcast engineer, and a recent graduate of the University of Hartford. I am a founding partner of SJI Media LLC, a company dedicated to production for performance ensembles, and the former Chief Engineer of the on-campus TV station STN2, and a broadcast engineer for NPR and PBS affiliate stations.

I am a lifelong musician, and I play French Horn and guitar professionally in all kinds of settings. In my free time, I am an avid composer, arranger, and band-leader! Compiled in this document are my resume, reference contacts, and work portfolio can be found here. If you have any questions, feel free to email me and I'll be happy to chat!

September 2021 - Present
Owner | Production Manager
SJI Media

SJI Media is a group initially formed to serve the needs of the community group, the Tidewater Winds, in order to allow them to perform while in lockdown. Now, SJI Media is an independent production company working with instrumental performance ensembles from around Virginia, specialising in concert production. We offer services in general audio capture, audio editing, live-sound reinforcement, broadcast video, and holistic concert production. Current proud partners include the Virginia Symphony, Symphonicity, Tidewater Winds, and the Hartt School of Music.

Director
Deep Blue

I am the director for the Deep Blue family of ensembles. The ensembles include a traditional big band and small groups which play music ranging from jazz, soul, rnb, funk, pop, and vgm. The group is aimed at expanding the reach of jazz education in the Hampton Roads area for high school and college students, partnering with professionals to bring a unique performance experience for musicians and audiences alike!

Engineering | Chief Engineer
STN Channel Two

STN Channel Two is an organisation that revolves around putting together a thirty minute news broadcast every Friday at 5PM during the regular school year, and has been constructed to be as real-to-life as is possible. As an engineer, I was typically A1 for broadcasts, and managed the Dante networking and live broadcast uplink. As Chief Engineer, I oversaw all technical aspects of the station's operation, including maintaing a series of edit suites, maintaining all studio and office computers in a local network, upkeep of ROSS Xpression and Inception, RUSHWORKS A-List, Avid NEXUS Data Servers, Dante Networking, as well as any other techincal needs the organisation has. My most recent project was building a truck-in-a-box in order to allow the organisation mobile streaming capabilities for campus events. Recently, I documented the inner workings of the entire studio, which you can learn about here. I also worked as a producer, working on most major national or political coverage, including in-depth coverage of the midterms this past year.
